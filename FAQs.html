Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about Solar, Generators, and Electrical Maintenance — answered.

What types of solar systems do you install?

We install grid-tied, off-grid, and hybrid solar systems designed for residential, commercial, and industrial applications tailored to your needs.

How long does a solar installation take?

Installations typically take between 1 to 3 days depending on system size and site specifics, with minimal disruption.

Do you provide maintenance for solar panels?

Yes, we offer regular cleaning and maintenance services to ensure your solar panels operate at peak efficiency year-round.

What kind of generators do you install?

We install petrol, diesel, and gas-powered generators from trusted brands, suitable for both residential backup and commercial power solutions.

How often should my generator be serviced?

Generators should be serviced at least once a year or after 100 operational hours to maintain reliability and performance.

What electrical maintenance services do you offer?

Our services include inspections, repairs, rewiring, fault finding, switchboard upgrades, and safety compliance checks.

Can you help with emergency electrical repairs?

Yes, we provide prompt emergency repair services to keep your electrical systems safe and operational.

Are your technicians certified?

All our technicians are fully qualified, certified, and experienced in solar, generator, and electrical systems.

How do I know if my electrical system needs maintenance?

Signs include flickering lights, frequent outages, tripped breakers, unusual smells or sounds, and outdated wiring. Contact us for an inspection.

Do you offer warranties on your installations?

Yes, we provide manufacturer warranties on equipment and workmanship guarantees on all our installations.
